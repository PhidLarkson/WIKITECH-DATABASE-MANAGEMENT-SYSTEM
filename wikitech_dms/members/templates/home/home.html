Welcome to Wikimedia Technical Group

This is a technical group affiliated with KNUST.

About Us

The Wikitech Club KNUST is a student-led organization at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology dedicated to fostering a community of passionate learners and innovators in the field of technology. We provide a platform for students to develop their skills, collaborate on projects, and gain exposure to the latest advancements in the tech industry.

Our club welcomes students from all academic backgrounds with an interest in technology. Whether you're a seasoned programmer, a budding entrepreneur, or simply curious about the potential of tech, we have something to offer you.

Mission & Vision

Mission:
  • To create a stimulating environment where students can explore their tech interests.
  • To equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the tech industry.
  • To foster collaboration and innovation among students with diverse tech backgrounds.
Vision:

To be a leading student organization at KNUST, empowering the next generation of tech leaders and entrepreneurs in Ghana and beyond.
